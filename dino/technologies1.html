Technologies

The new Dino-Lite wireless solution, the one-of-a-kind wireless microscope concept, is designed and engineered to meet the growing needs of mobile and field applications. Without compromising the image quality and functionalities, Dino-Lite remodeled the handheld microscopes by adding wireless compatibility on its new breed - the AF series.

Axial Illumination

(AXI)

Casting light through the lens, the built-in and software controllable AXI is designed to provide axial or brightfield illumination, making it ideal for observing reflective surface under high magnification.

Extended Depth of Field

(EDOF)

EDOF increases the depth of field by stacking pictures of different focuses with one click.

Extended Dynamic Range

(EDR)

EDR increases the dynamic range by stacking pictures taken at different exposure with one click.

Flexible LED Control

(FLC)

With 4-quadrants switching capability and 6-levels luminance adjustability, the FLC provides greater controllability over the built-in LEDs.

Automatic Magnification Reading

(AMR)

Enables the software to read the magnification from the device automatically, the AMR unburdens the measurement tasks and improves the accuracy of measurement.

Adaptable Interface

(USB / Wi-Fi Connectivity)

Answering the growing needs of mobile and field applications, the AF series is designed to provide wireless adaptability without compromising its image quality and functionalities.
