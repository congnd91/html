About us

Recruit.net allows job seekers to instantly find millions of jobs from thousands of web sites with a single search. We deliver job listings from corporate web sites, job boards, recruitment agencies and numerous other sources.

We perform more than a million job searches per day and operate 18 localized websites, in 6 major languages.

We work closely with job sites and companies to increase visibility and exposure for their job listings by sending job seekers directly to the jobs on their web site. If you would like us to add your job content please click here to submit the details for consideration. In order to maintain data integrity and quality for our users we only include jobs from reputable sources which meet a consistent high level of quality.

As a business Recruit.net generates revenue through our Pay-Per-Click recruitment advertising network which delivers advertisers highly targeted job seeker traffic and sponsored listings on our search results pages, together with detailed analytics and tracking.
