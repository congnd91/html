About us

Globargo is the world’s first Transport Network Community focused on the Consumer electronic market. Our speciality, same day and next day Express deliveries, for Mobile Phones, Tablets, Computers, Consoles, Televisions, small appliances, IT, spare parts and Audio.

By partnering with world’s top couriers such as UPS, FedEx, DHL, DB Schenker we can guarantee the security, speed of delivery and quality service that are an essential business needs in our industry. What makes truly unique to Globargo is our unique business model and company structure. Essentially, our clients are, at the same time, our business partners. By leveraging the size of our community and our multinational, de-centralize structure, we can offer our clients/partners the absolute best rates for express transport available in the market.

With over 14 years industry experience in the transport sector, thousands of transactions and multidisciplinary team, we have the know how, credibility and relationships to successfully execute the commitments of our most demanding client/partners.

MISSION

Our Mission is quite simple and straightforward:
To offer the best Express Delivery rates available in the market to our community
pared with a second to none 24/7 service experience.

Services

Express

We move goods in optimum conditions with the procedures and security suited to our industry requirements. Our strength are Express Services, same day or next day deliveries, across Europe for all sorts of Consumer Electronics, both road and air depending of the needs for each specific shipment.

Freight

Managing up to 4.000 Kg and multiple pallets per shipments, we can present and coordinate outstanding services for Freight transport with in-depth, expert knowledge of individual geographic trades and markets.

International

On demand, we can also cater services non-EU or international needs including customs clearance leveraging our international network of logistics partners which have been verified and tested and with whom we have been working for many years. With over 27 partners across the world, we count with offices and warehouses in Miami, New York, Singapore, Dubai and Hong Kong.

HOW IT WORKS

In a nutshell and effectively, we have created multiple omnibus accounts, where we group all the orders of our network into one unique massive, master account. By doing so, we can greatly decrease transport costs and, in turn, offer exceptional transport fees to our community than otherwise would be possible. Since we only work with top couriers, we ensure maximum security and efficiency for our deliveries, letting you sleep well at night.

This is a win-win-win situation for all parties which is fundamental for the long-term sustainability of the company. For once, couriers benefit from a reduce number of of accounts, decreasing administration and service costs, eliminating payments defaults defaults while increasing their overall volumes. Globargo positions itself as innovative market leader in the transport market for consumer electronics. And finally, our end clients enjoy a better pricing for their transport needs.
