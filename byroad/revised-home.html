How It Works?

Enjoy bidding on products you want on Drivvia

Browse through thousands of products across a wide range of categories on Drivvia and bid on products that you want to get at a great value.

Win the auction after the seller accepts your bid

Win the auction by entering a fair price for the products you want. Once the seller accepts the bid your details will be shared with the seller so you can complete the purchase.

Contact the seller and purchase the product

After the auction ends, co-ordinate the sale and delivery of the product directly with the seller. make sure to check the condition of the product and all other specifications.

For Buyers - Get the price right, everytime.

  • Get better value on products you buy

  • No middlemen. Deal directly with the seller

  • You choose the bid amount, giving you complete control of your purchase value

  • Live bidding makes buying fun

  • View the savings on every product in real time

  • Communicate instantly with the seller and complete your purchase after winning a bid

For Sellers - Get better value for your listing

  • Listing your products is quick, efficient and 100% FREE

  • Get a fair market price for the items you want to sell

  • Accept bids easily on our platform

  • Get your listing verified by our quality experts

  • Simple fee of AED 399 on winning bids. No hidden charges.

  • All purchases are closed within 7 days of winning a bid
