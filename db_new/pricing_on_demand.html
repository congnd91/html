Frequently Asked Questions

Does DesignBold charge a sign-up fee?

No. When you sign up for DesignBold’s Pro plan, you only pay monthly or yearly for your subscription. Similarly, there is no additional charge associated with coins.

What payment forms does DesignBold accept?

DesignBold accepts all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, JCB, Discover Card, and Diners Club.

If I use a stock photo outside of DesignBold’s premium stock storage, will you charge me extra money?

Yes. If you use a stock photo outside our premium stock storage, you need to purchase using coins. Coins can be bought with our bundles.

If I cancel my plan early, will I be refunded?

Unfortunately, it isn’t possible to cancel a Pro plan that you already paid for and get a refund. If you choose monthly plan, the cancellation will take effect by the end of the already-paid month. You will not be charged after that month. Similarly, if you choose yearly plan, the cancellation will take effect at the end of the already-paid year, and you will not be charged after that year.

Will I be charged if I sign up for the 30 Days Trial?

If you sign up for our 30 Days Trial, you can enjoy 30 days of our Pro membership totally FREE of charge. After 30 days end, your account will stay at our Pro membership and you will be automatically charged.

If you'd rather not become a Pro member, you can cancel anytime. We'll remind you when your free trial is about to end and you can make the decision to continue or not. If you cancel this trial anytime within 30 days, you will not be charged.
