Company Profile

ISS is dedicated to developing and manufacturing various types of optical media applications, not only the digital data but also radio frequency signal solution. With the combined concepts of technological innovation and green energy, we believe in the values of outstanding service, top-notch quality, and integration. Task team with more then 20 years of optical related filed and marketing experience, our team is able to tailor for the fast-changing needs to the markets in order to create ideal and competitive products/solutions through innovation and integration.
