About us

Human resource is always been the fundamental of our group. Most of the time when people look back to their entire life and found that they did not truly recognize who they really are. Not to mention the young employees would know exactly how they are going to play their role in his/her career

When the new employees joined YC Group, I always told them to evaluate themselves if this is the job you want during the first 3 month. You should quit it immediately if it’s not what you have in mind and move on for the next opportunity. Life is short; there is not much time to be wasted. As a supervisor, you have to offer your help to the new employees by finding themselves.
