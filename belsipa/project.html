Education

Covered by its statutory goals BELSIPA strives to facilitate and contribute to any investor and salesforce education initiative by banks and/or sector associations whenever these relate to structured products.

UPDATE

In 2020 BELSIPA will again offer with FEBELFIN Academy a redesigned one-day course on structured products as alternative asset class. We invite you to preregister at FEBELFIN Academy‘s course pages so that you get an invitation to the course once a date is fixed. Please use this link (French) or this link (Dutch).

Please also regularly check this page for updates or subscribe to our LinkedIn site.


HISTORY

In 2016 and 2017 FEBELFIN Academy and BELSIPA jointly offered for the first time regular training courses on the manufacturing and distribution of structured products in Belgium. The courses were aimed at professionals dealing with structured products in Belgian financial institutions. Attendees received professional credit points.
