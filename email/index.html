Digital Strategy Tip

Form a robust digital segmentation data strategy and plan to acquire data. Use these insights across owned digital properties and paid media empowering more nuanced digital consumer journeys.

Digital campaign tip

“Consistency is Key”- with more and more platforms leveraging machine learning to optimise campaign performance, adopting a consistent approach to optimisation and avoiding erratic step changes is essential to get the most out of the algorithm. By planning for incremental expansion you allow the machine to adapt and evolve without sending shockwaves into the system.
