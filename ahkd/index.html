The Best of  Both Worlds

Cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin have opened up a world of innovation, with new opportunities for business, investment and personal finance. But how to move between cash and crypto and make the most of your money is not always easy. AHKD offers the answer.

Just like cryptocurrency, AHKD draws on the benefits of blockchain. You can transfer money easily, for hardly any cost, and it’s secure. But as a stablecoin, pegged to the Hong Kong Dollar, AHKD is also stable and it can easily be converted to other currencies or back to cash.

AHKD CORE FEATURES

Stable

Each AHKD is backed at a 1:1 ratio by the Hong Kong Dollar.

Transparent

Held in an independent Trust, cash reserves benefit from regular attestations to ensure proof of funds.

Secure

Minted and secured on the Ethereum blockchain, AHKD offers transparency and privacy at once.

Global

Seamlessly transfer funds anywhere or trade AHKD against major crypto assets such as Bitcoin.

Reach Your Financial Goals

Whether for holding, trading, commerce, remittances or payments, AHKD makes it easy to navigate between the traditional fiat and the digital asset economy.

AHKD OPPORTUNITIES

Blockchain Firms

Adopt AHKD to reach new markets, price goods and services in line with your client base, and save on operational costs.

Crypto Exchanges

Use AHKD to expand across fiat on and off ramps into crypto and optimize cross-exchange settlement.

Market Makers

Use AHKD as your base currency, inventory, or as an instrument for counterparty clearance.

Individuals

AHKD enables opportunities for portfolio diversification, trading, arbitrage, and ownership.

Balance is key

We’ve designed the process of subscription and redemption as easy as possible. The overall process is designed to always maintain a proper balance between the circulating supply of AHKD and the Hong Kong Dollar held in the Trust, to back the stablecoin.
