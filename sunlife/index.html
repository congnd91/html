What’s in a financial roadmap?

Your financial roadmap shows you where you are today and how you can get where you want to go. It’s based on what you tell us about your financial situation. This includes income, assets, monthly expenses, insurance coverage, as well as your debt. It makes assumptions based on interest rates and investment returns to project your future wealth

Your financial roadmap will help you meet your needs today and in retirement. As well, it can help you protect yourself from the unexpected along the way. It shows how investments and insurance can work together to help you meet your financial goals

You can make decisions that will change the outcome down the road. You could save more, spend less, retire later, change your investment mix and/or reduce debt. With your financial roadmap and the help of a Sun Life Financial representative, you can be confident that you are on the path to reach your goals.

How often do I update my Sun Life Compass roadmap?

We recommend you update it twice a year. It’s also wise to do when you’ve had a major life event, for example, marriage, job change, birth of a child, inheritance or divorce.

How do I know if my financial roadmap is right for me?

Your financial roadmap makes suggestions on how you could use your money to achieve the things that are important to you now, and the things you want in the future. It shows you how you could balance your priorities. For example, you may be saving for a house, repaying debt, funding a child’s education, planning for retirement or protecting yourself and your loved ones.
