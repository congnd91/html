Install for Chrome

The DesignBold Chrome Extension is only available to Google Chrome Browser Follow these simple steps or watch the video below for more details:

1. Right-click on image, and choose Open image with DesignBold on the context menu. OR click on the Hover button that automatically attach on the image that you hover over
2. Design your image using our tools and then click on Download
3. For additional options, right click on DesignBold icon on your Chrome browser extension bar and choose Option. To learn more about how these options work, click here
4. Set your Default document type, and change your Hover button’s settings how you see fit
