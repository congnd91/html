Introduction

DesignIT Button

The DesignIt button is one of the easiest ways to have a full-fledged design editor on your app or platform without the need to build your own tool. It is an integration tool that allows website owners to give their users designing ability, utilizing DesignBold's platform.

* currently available on Web only.


Use Cases

DesignIt Button is used to enable the following experiences:

Well developed tool to help you grow

We are a powerful design tool that allows your users to design freely on your website using our library of templates, fonts and high quality stock photos. So without investing time, human resources,ystem, facilities and money, you now have a tool on your website, enhance experience and attract more users in the long term. That way, you can focus on the product, we'll take care of the design.

Seamless workflow

Make your platform an all-in-one place with DesignBold, better user experience leads to higher engagement and conversion rate.

If your website is only offering “Upload" option, chances are your users would have to design images elsewhere, using different tools to manipulate images, then return to your site to upload. You can lose users along the process. Wouldn’t it be nice to have a design tool inside your platform without the need to build one of your own? Your users can access our design editor via an Iframe without having to leave your site. The final product can then be previewed or published directly back to your page.

DesignIt Button can be integrated directly to your backend or SDK.

Content creation

Instead of just offering “Upload” option, DesignIt Button also gives your users power to design. Let your users express themselves not only through texts but also visual content. The button allows them quickly and easily create stunning graphic designs utilizing 10,000+ of beautiful layouts from social media, headers, ads, CV, presentation, etc ....

FEATURES

Quick sign up

Instead of having your users go through our standard sign up process, (maybe include cả 1 ảnh long process vào đây), you can opt-in to our quick sign-up option.

For optimal user experience, we can automatically register an account for users with the information provided by the partner.

Storage

DesignBold currently offer unlimited storage for all users. All designs created by your users will be stored in WorkSpace under their DesignBold's account. Users can can come back here and access, edit, manage or delete designs at any time. Only designs they decide to publish will be visible to the public.

Collaboration and sharing

DesignBold allows users to share their design drafts with their friends/colleagues, who can also contribute or add an edit to the designs. (within the DesignBold easy-to-use interface)

n addition, users can also share their designs on different social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, etc …

Content resource

Provide your users with more diversified resource and integrate with us. DesignBold has a library of 10,000+ layouts & templates, over 100 million stock photos with license and other premium elements. We support over 60 standardized doctype including Facebook cover, headers, ads, twitters, etsy, instagram posts etc …

Custom dimension is also available. Users can also create a design canvas with their own dimensions by pixels, mm, or inches. This is particularly helpful when designing a graphic for a blog or a website.

Image editing

Once an user has uploaded their image(s), they can apply filters, use cropping tools, add premium elements and texts. Click here for a quick demo.

In addition, we render images in high and printable resolution.

Text editing

Any part of the design can be easily edited. Users have the ability to edit texts from fonts, size, colors to even upload their own fonts. This is particularly useful when users use languages other than English. For more information on font upload, please click here.


App Review

Create a New App

By creating a new App, DesignBold creates an App ID which is an object that represents your actual application and which stores settings and information for it. A DesignBold App ID is created that represents your app.

To request an AppID, please sign in with your credentials at https://developers.designbold.com/ select Create a New App.

App Review

Once your AppRequest has been submitted, our team will review your information to determine whether you are a good match

Our team will get back to you from 3-5 business days. If you haven’t heard from us after that, please send an email to integration@designbold.com

Your AppID and AppSecret will be available immediately after AppRequest on https://developers.designbold.com/ , however, your app will not be activated until we have approved your information.
