Why you should never travel without travel insurance?

Travel Insurance for independent travellers. Nobody wants to think about all the things that might go wrong on their trip, but these things can and do happen.

AloTrip Travel insurance has been designed by travellers to cover everything you need. We make sure you’re covered, even if you run out of travel insurance on the road. We don't just keep you insured, with us you'll travel smarter and safer.

10 REASONS TO HAVE TRAVEL INSURANCE

1. No travel insurance – No Schengen Visa

All plan of Travel Flex can adapt visa application requirements:

· Medical expenses benefit limits from 39.000€ to 78.000€

2. Who can help when there has suddenly risk?

Worldwide Emergency assist benefit includes:

· Emergency Evacuation: Unlimited
· Repatriation: Unlimited
· Hospital Expenses Guarantee up to ~78.000€
· Additional Costs of Travel & Accommodation
· Assistance Services

3. The food of countryside in China is delicious but it may not good for your health, even can lead to food poisoning. You can go to the nearest hospital and the medical fee is really expensive. Worldwide Medical Expenses benefit includes:

· Medical Expenses;
· Follow-up Care;
· Emergency Evacuation;
· Hospital Expenses Guarantee;
· Hospital Cash Allowance …etc

4. Travel Flex reimburse for the accident which happen when joining in to the adventuring entertainment game.

Condition

· Not cover accident whilst engaging in any sports or game in a professional capacity or organized contact sports
· Rock or mountain climbing which is not higher than 5000 meters, scuba diving to a sea-depth of greater than 20 meters.

5. John - 30 years old, has permanent disability after an accident happened in a 10 days business trip to Singapore. Who will be responsible for financial problem to cover the cost of medical fee and for take caring of John in the next few years? Personal Accident benefit cover for accidental death or permanent disability including loss of one or more limbs or loss of sight in one or both eyes. The limit of Personal Accident up to ~250.000$

6. After an earthquake happened in Japan on 11th March 2011, you need to cancel a trip to Japan which planning for a long time. All cost expenses for airlines ticket, hotel, tour plan which you already paid the deposit will hardly returned.

Curtailment of Trip/ Cancellation Charges:

Travel Flex reimburse for irrecoverable prepaid travel arrangement deposits or any increased cost of travel.

For example: deposit fee for airlines ticket, hotel, tour, loading fee for transportation, accommodation…etc

7. You stay over 32 hours by plane to Toronto, Canada and when arriving, you could not receive the luggage. The airlines informed that your luggage has lost and will deliver after 1 days with the time planned. You need to do a connecting flight to Halifax for joining in a very important meeting and could not wait for that. You will need to use your personal things and clothes.

Baggage Delay benefit:

· Baggage Delay: Emergency purchases of essential items of toiletries and clothing when the checked baggage is delayed at destination abroad.
· Maximum limit: ~ 60 USD /item

8. Loss your passport, cameras, laptops, money, baggage because of robbery, thief or airline

Baggage and Personal Effects benefit reimburse for loss or damage from:

· Accident, theft, burglary, robbery or mishandling by carriers: maximum limit up to VND30.000.000 ~
· Travel Document: maximum limit up to ~ 20,000 USD;
· Limit ~ 250 USD/ item and ~ 500 USD/ pair or set

9. Your trip to Hong Kong about 31 days. Your big business partner in Viet Nam passed away after 1 weeks you have left VN. You will need to come back VN for visiting the partner’s family and managing amicable everything for coming back HK.

Incidental Home Country benefit:

· Unlimited time for returning to the country of origin
· Total returning days not over 14 days

10. When travel to Australia with your family, your son broke the window of the supermarket.

Luckily, your son has travel insurance which included Personal Liability benefit.

Personal Liability benefit

· Travel Flex reimburse for Indemnity against legal liability to a third party as a result of accidental injury or loss or damage to property.
· Maximum limit: 100,000 USD
