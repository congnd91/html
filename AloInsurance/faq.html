Are Vietnam Airlines hand baggage regulations applied for all flights?

Hand baggage

Yes. Vietnam Airlines applies regulations on hand baggage for all flights and routes as follows: 02 pieces for passengers of Business Class and 01 piece for passengers of Economy Class. Each piece weighs no more than 7kg, and the sum of three dimensions does not exceed 115cm (equal to 56 x 36 x 23 cm or 22 x 14 x 9 inches). If your hand baggage overreaches the quantity, weight and dimensions as mentioned above, the airline shall refuse to carry your baggage as carry-on baggage, and order the baggage to be transported as checked baggage.
