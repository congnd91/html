Exercises, Challenges and badges!

The PentesterLab PRO content is organised as badge. A badge is composed of multiple exercises or challenges. To get a badge, you need to finish all the exercises for this badge.

Once you finish a badge, you will receive a certificate of completion.

Scoring an exercise!

There are different ways to score an exercise. You may:

Get a key that you have to copy in the main website

Get to run a command to score (/usr/local/bin/score ba23d943-d297-443a-9a88-e0269230c826)
