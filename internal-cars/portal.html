InternalCars Portal

InternalCars Remarketing-system är Sveriges bästa system för Generalagenter, Finansbolag och Leasingbolag.

Ett professionellt system för test, hantering och försäljning av interna bilar tex. press, demo, hyr- och tjänstebilar mot egna ÅF. Optimalt för hantering av leasing- och privatleasing flödet.

Our BegTest system simplifies and modernizes the reception and assessment of vehicles in your workflow. You get an online test archive where all tests are always easily accessible from computer, tablet or on the go on your mobile. The tests have real car pictures and damage pictures that are easily captured directly into the system via tablet or smartphone. Smart features around the test protocol save time and money such as our automatically completed Glass Notification, VDN Protocols, PDFs, or simple Valuation Certificates etc.

With InternalCar's bidding system , you can set up processes for bidding, countdown auctions, open or hidden sales, sales at optional prices, Instant Cash Offer solutions and also send to the industry's largest auction companies. You quickly reach all the country's car dealers, which guarantees that you really get the best deal on the exchange, or your stock car.
