About us

Professional, Effective Test and Evaluation of Switch-up and Used Cars.

InternalCars BegTest is the leading service for testing and evaluating cars, and directly connected to the Dealer Offer.

Use BegTest to streamline your internal handling, to get the best value from replacement cars and to buy the car at the Dealer's Bid Marketplace

For professional handling of used cars at Sweden's car dealers




Do you handle replacement cars, internal cars or leases of leasing vehicles?

InternalCars Handlarbudet constantly drives and further develops the market's best service for testing, evaluating, assessing and forwarding test protocols, product declarations and used certificates for used cars. Use our service also to get the best price for the vehicle if it is to be forwarded for trading to other dealers - we have automatic links to the leading marketplaces including Handlarbudet.se for bidding even before the exchange transaction is complete.

Almost 200,000 vehicles are handled annually for a large number of car dealers, general agents and leasing companies. Our users are present at more than 1,400 locations throughout Sweden.

Together with the Swedish Vehicle Workshop Association, we also offer the support service for Certified Testman - training and quality assurance of used tests.

InternalCars Handlarbudet is a privately owned company with an eye for continuous development and improvement of our services together with Sweden's car dealers. Our goal is to be Best at BegTest - and all peripheral services that come before and after used vehicles are assessed and documented. We strive to offer the broadest and most automated basic information, and the most useful testing protocol - so you can build your professional and efficient processes.
