EVENT VENUE
Hotel Warszawianka Centrum Kongresowe

Hotel Warszawianka, located by the Zalew Zegrzyński ca. 40 minute drive from the centre of Warsaw, is a harmonious combination of modern convention centre with a hotel providing the whole family with comfortable rest.

The Hotel Warszawianka Convention Centre is one of the most professional and functional interiors of this type. In 37 rooms you may organise congresses, conferences, fairs, banquets, training courses and all special events. The largest room – the Convention Hall, 6 metres high – can seat 1,500 guests.

• 300 Rooms
• 23 meeting and conference auditoriums
• Private beach area
• Gym, disco, restaurants...
• Located by the Zegrzyński Lake
• 30 minutes from Warsaw
• Recreational water sport area
• Natural surroundings
