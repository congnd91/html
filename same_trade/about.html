A New and Exciting Brokerage in Forex Trading

Samtrade FX is an all new online trading brokerage in providing Forex Trading and other related services. We are founded with one clear objective – to provide traders with safe, easy, as well as low cost access to foreign exchange markets. Our founders and partners are all traders themselves and have extensive trading experience in Forex and CFD. At the same time, our shareholders were all reputable figures in the financial industry. Our Advisory Board, with their extensive knowledge and experience, is able to provide our clients with unrivaled professional training and advice.

TSamtrade FX is incorporated under registered number 14680 by the Registrar of International Companies (International Company Act, CAP 222) from the Republic of Vanuatu. Samtrade FX possesses the principal's license and is authorized to carry on the business of dealing in securities under the Dealers in Securities (Licensing) Act (CAP 70) from the Republic of Vanuatu. We conduct our business in Forex and CFD trading through our website – www.samtradefx.com, which is a fully independent STP/ECN platform for both retail and institutional traders.

Since its incorporation, Samtrade FX has developed an extended list of both retail and institutional clients. As a fully licensed brokerage, we meet a reliable standard of corporate governance. We firmly believe that we have the best qualified personal to ensure a safe and secure trading environment that is uniquely yours. We have regional offices in Singapore, Hong Kong as well as Shanghai to provide an all-round, personalized service to our clients and partners. Most importantly, your trading experience is important to us and therefore forms the core of our ideology of intelligent investments. We strive to set the highest standards of client satisfaction and Cater to the special needs of clients.
