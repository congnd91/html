產品介紹

The main business items of manufacturing and sales for the Group are Double-sided and Multi-layer Printed Circuit Board (PCB). The products are mainly exported to Japan, Korea, Europe and America to major consumer electronics manufacturers, including Canon Inc., Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd., Technicolor and Sharp Corporation, etc.
