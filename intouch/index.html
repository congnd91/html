Getting Started

Before the initial session, we will have 20-minute phone consultation to determine your therapeutic needs and how I can be of service to you. During our phone consult you may ask me any questions to help you make your decision in working together. This consult is free of charge. Booking can be determined during the consultation process to ensure our schedules align. The first session after the phone consultation will take 75 minutes
