Receipts will be provided upon request. I offer a sliding scale for those in financial need and I have allocated a percentage of my practice to such cases. Please contact me to inquire and I will advise you if this aspect of my practice is full. In a case like this, a waiting list is available.

Cancellation Policy: A minimum of 48 hours is required to cancel with no financial penalty. If I do not receive 48 hours’ notice for cancelled appointments a full fee for your appointment will be required. Cancellations less than this time frame, for reasons other than illness or emergency, will be charged full rate. Please be mindful that your appointments are scheduled especially for you.
